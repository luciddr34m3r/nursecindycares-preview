The Framework

The 7 Types of Rest

Most people think rest means sleep. But research shows there are seven distinct types of rest — and being deficient in any one of them leads to exhaustion that no amount of sleep can fix.

01

Physical

Passive (sleep, naps) and active (yoga, stretching) rest that repairs the body and restores physical energy.

02

Mental

Quieting the thinking, analyzing mind — especially vital for high performers and decision-makers.

03

Social

Identifying which relationships restore vs. drain you, and creating space for authentic connection.

04

Creative

The space that allows inspiration, wonder, and imagination to replenish — essential for innovation.

05

Emotional

Permission to feel without performing — releasing the exhaustion of always having to be "okay."

06

Sensory

Reducing the overload of screens, noise, and constant stimulation that depletes our nervous system.

07

Spiritual

Connection to purpose, meaning, and something beyond the day-to-day — the foundation of resilience.
