Before You Ask

Common Questions

Does Nurse Cindy travel?

Yes — she's available for in-person engagements nationally and virtually worldwide. Travel availability varies by season; reach out early for date-specific requests.

Can topics be customized?

Absolutely. Each engagement is tailored to the specific audience, industry, and outcomes you're hoping to achieve. No generic keynotes here.

What's the lead time for booking?

2–4 weeks is ideal for most events. For large conferences or specific date requirements, earlier is better. Inquire to check availability.

What size audiences does she work with?

From intimate leadership retreats of 15 to conference rooms of 500+. Format and facilitation approach are adapted for group size.
